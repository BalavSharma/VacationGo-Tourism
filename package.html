6-Days Tour

5 Nights | 6 Days
Bhutan 6-Day Journey
Day 1
Arrival in Paro
  • Arrive at Paro International Airport and meet your guide.
  • Check into your hotel and unwind.
  • Visit Paro Dzong (Rinpung Dzong) and the National Museum (Ta Dzong).
  • Stroll Paro town and enjoy local cuisine at a traditional restaurant.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 2
Paro - Hike to Tiger's Nest Monastery
  • Early breakfast followed by a 2-3 hour hike to Tiger's Nest Monastery.
  • Explore the monastery and enjoy panoramic valley views.
  • Return to Paro for lunch.
  • Visit Kyichu Lhakhang, one of Bhutan's oldest temples.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 3
Paro to Thimphu
  • Drive from Paro to Thimphu (about 1.5 hours).
  • Visit Buddha Dordenma and the National Memorial Chorten.
  • Tour Tashichho Dzong (after 5 pm on weekdays).
  • Evening stroll through local markets and shops.
  • Overnight in Thimphu.
Day 4
Thimphu to Punakha
  • Drive to Punakha (about 2.5 hours) via Dochula Pass with a stop at Druk Wangyal Chortens.
  • Explore Chimi Lhakhang (the fertility temple).
  • Visit Punakha Dzong at the confluence of two rivers.
  • Walk across the Punakha Suspension Bridge.
  • Enjoy a traditional Bhutanese dinner.
  • Overnight in Punakha.
Day 5
Punakha to Paro
  • Hike to Khamsum Yulley Namgyal Chorten with optional rafting.
  • Drive back to Paro (about 3 hours).
  • Relax with a cultural program or traditional dance.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 6
Departure
  • Enjoy last-minute shopping or sightseeing based on flight time.
  • Departure with lasting Bhutan memories.

Bhutan 7-Day Journey
Day 1
Arrival in Paro - Drive to Thimphu
  • Arrive at Paro International Airport and meet your guide.
  • Scenic 1.5-hour drive to Thimphu with a stop at Tamchog Lhakhang Iron Bridge.
  • Check into your hotel and unwind.
  • Evening walk around Thimphu town and Craft Bazaar for local textiles and crafts.
  • Overnight in Thimphu.
Day 2
Explore Thimphu
  • Memorial Chorten for a spiritual atmosphere with locals.
  • Buddha Dordenma for panoramic valley views.
  • Folk Heritage Museum or Textile Museum for culture and craftsmanship.
  • Tashichho Dzong after office hours.
  • Overnight in Thimphu.
Day 3
Thimphu to Punakha
  • Drive to Punakha (about 3 hours) via Dochula Pass with 108 chortens and Himalayan views.
  • Visit Chimi Lhakhang (Fertility Temple).
  • Continue to Punakha Dzong at the river confluence.
  • Overnight in Punakha.
Day 4
Punakha to Paro
  • Optional rafting on Mo Chhu or hike to Khamsum Yulley Namgyal Chorten.
  • Drive back to Paro (4-5 hours) with a stop at Simtokha Dzong.
  • Visit Rinpung Dzong and Ta Dzong (National Museum) in Paro.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 5
Hike to Tiger's Nest
  • Hike to Taktsang (Tiger's Nest) Monastery, perched on a cliff at 3,120m.
  • Approx. 5-hour round trip with tea stop at the Taktsang Cafeteria.
  • Return to Paro and unwind with a Bhutanese hot stone bath.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 6
Explore Paro Valley
  • Visit Kyichu Lhakhang and Drukgyel Dzong ruins.
  • Enjoy a farmhouse lunch and connect with a local family.
  • Try archery or shop for handwoven textiles, incense, and tea.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 7
Departure
  • Airport transfer from Paro for your onward flight.

Bhutan 9-Day Journey
Day 1
Arrival in Paro - Drive to Thimphu
  • Arrive at Paro International Airport with views of Himalayan peaks.
  • Drive to Thimphu (about 1.5 hours) along the river valleys.
  • Stop at Tamchog Lhakhang Iron Bridge built by Thangtong Gyalpo.
  • Evening stroll along Norzin Lam for handicrafts and souvenirs.
  • Overnight in Thimphu.
Day 2
Thimphu Sightseeing
  • Buddha Dordenma with 125,000 smaller Buddhas.
  • Memorial Chorten for daily prayers and circumambulation.
  • Folk Heritage Museum showcasing rural life.
  • Tashichho Dzong, the government seat and summer residence of the Je Khenpo.
  • Overnight in Thimphu.
Day 3
Thimphu to Punakha
  • Drive via Dochula Pass (3,100m) and its 108 chortens with Himalayan views.
  • Visit Chimi Lhakhang (Fertility Temple).
  • Explore Punakha Dzong at the confluence of Pho Chhu and Mo Chhu.
  • Walk the Pho Chhu Suspension Bridge.
  • Overnight in Punakha.
Day 4
Punakha to Bumthang
  • Drive via Pele La Pass (3,300m) into central Bhutan.
  • Stop at Trongsa Dzong, Bhutan's largest fortress.
  • Continue to Bumthang, the spiritual heartland.
  • Overnight in Bumthang.
Day 5
Bumthang Sightseeing
  • Kurje Lhakhang with Guru Rinpoche's body imprint.
  • Jambay Lhakhang, one of Bhutan's oldest temples.
  • Tamshing Lhakhang and its ancient murals.
  • Swiss Farm for local cheese, honey, and apple brandy.
  • Overnight in Bumthang.
Day 6
Bumthang Excursion
  • Mebar Tsho (Burning Lake) pilgrimage site.
  • Ura Valley village visit.
  • Tharpaling Monastery for peaceful views.
  • Return to Bumthang town for a relaxed evening.
  • Overnight in Bumthang.
Day 7
Bumthang to Punakha
  • Option 1: 35-minute scenic flight to Paro (if available).
  • Option 2: Drive with stops in Trongsa and Wangdue.
  • Evening at leisure in Punakha.
  • Overnight in Punakha.
Day 8
Drive to Paro
  • Drive back to Paro through countryside and rice terraces.
  • Visit Kyichu Lhakhang and Drukgyel Dzong ruins.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 9
Hike to Tiger's Nest
  • Hike to Taktsang (Tiger's Nest) Monastery perched on a cliff at 3,120m.
  • Approx. 5-hour round trip with tea stop at the Taktsang Cafeteria.
  • Return to Paro and relax with a Bhutanese hot stone bath.
  • Overnight in Paro.
Day 10
Departure
  • Transfer to Paro International Airport for your onward flight.
